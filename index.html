Automated Summation

Past

Customers were expected to fill deposit slip,give it to the tellers who cross-check what they filled in deposit slip as against their cash.

Present

Customer walks directly to the tellers with their cash, teller collects and use an A4 paper to record the counted cash so as not to make mistakes with bulk cash, use calculator to compute total figure before inputing on the system.

Future

Customer walks directly to the tellers with their cash, teller collects and input counted figures directly on a designated menu on CBA, total cash migrates directly to the cash deposit boc thereafter.
